How to Play

Use the following keys:

Right Arrow Move right
Left Arrow Move left
Space Jump
A Attack

The wizard (Myrddin) has been cursed by an evil troll who has taken over his kingdom with knights. To free the kingdom, the wizard must fight against the knights and ultimately defeat the troll. Goal of the game: The magician must collect various magical artifacts that come in the form of coins or crystals. These artifacts give him new abilities that eventually make him strong enough to defeat the final boss.
